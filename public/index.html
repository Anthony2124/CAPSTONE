Welcome to D.I.E.T.S. Ecosystem

The D.I.E.T.S. (Diagnostic Integration & Electronic Treatment System) is a centralized portal for Aurora Memorial Hospital. This platform is designed to make your hospital visits smoother, safer, and more coordinated.

1. Schedule Appointments

Request consultations, lab tests, or dietary planning in advance using the Appointment Form to get your Queue Token.

2. Streamlined Records

Your health records, lab results, and medications are automatically linked for our medical staff to view in real-time.

3. Diet & Therapy Locks

Our system safeguards your nutrition by automatically locking specific diets based on your critical lab results (e.g., Diabetic/Renal profiles).

4. PhilHealth Integration

Faster billing calculations with automatic PhilHealth Case Rate deductions directly processed from your diagnostics.
